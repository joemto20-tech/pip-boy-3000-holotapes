### Description

Scavenge mutfruit, grow longer, and keep your bloodworm alive by avoiding walls,
your own tail, and your opponent (vs mode).

### Controls

#### Start / Game-over screen

- Turn either knob to toggle between **1P** (solo) and **2P** (Versus) mode.
- Click the left knob to start (or restart) a round.
- Click the left knob from the game over screen to play again.

### Instructions

Eat the mutfruit to grow. Don't bite yourself. Don't run into the walls. In 2P,
don't bite your opponent. The game speeds up every 5 mutfruits eaten.

#### Solo Mode (1P)

- Turn the left knob (or right knob) to steer.

#### Versus Mode (2P)

- Player 1: turn the left knob to steer.
- Player 2: turn the right knob to steer.
- If a player crashes, the other player continues to play until they crash as
  well.
- The player with the higher score at the end wins.
