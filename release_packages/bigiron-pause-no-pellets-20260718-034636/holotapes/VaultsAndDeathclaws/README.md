# Vaults & Deathclaws

### Info

- [@CodyTolene](https://github.com/CodyTolene)

### Description

Vaults & Deathclaws is a Vault-Tec companion for your Pip-Boy. Boot it up to a
vault intro reel, then manage Fallout RPG character sheets directly on the
device: every field from the official sheet (S.P.E.C.I.A.L., skills, combat,
damage resistance with per-location injuries, current radiation, caps, carry
weight, equipment, conditions, companions, notes, and the weapons / ammo / gear
/ perks tables), saved to the SD card.

### Controls

Startup Screen (Intro Video/Animation):

- Left knob press to skip.

Main Screen (Character Sheets List):

- Left knob press to select.
- Left knob hold to delete.
- Left knob scroll to move vertically.
- Right knob scroll to move horizontally.

Keyboard:

- Left knob press to select.
- Left knob hold for caps lock.
- Left knob scroll to move vertically.
- Right knob scroll to move horizontally.

Edit Screens (Character Sheet Editor):

- Left knob press to select.
- Left knob scroll to move vertically.
- Right knob scroll to move horizontally (buttons).
- Right knob scroll to increase or decrease values on rows.

Dice Roller:

- Left knob scroll to move vertically.
- Right knob scroll to move horizontally.
- Left knob press to select / toggle / open.

### Instructions

Create, edit, and save character sheets. Open the dice roller to roll dice.
